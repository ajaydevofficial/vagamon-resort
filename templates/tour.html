\ Luxurious Stay \


These cozy rooms are designed to give you a great night’s sleep with an orthopaedic mattress, excellent sound insulation and near-perfect black-out curtains. The rooms offer you ‘close to home’ comfort and keep that spring in your step, when you travel on business.The Cotttage can accomodate maximum of 8 members with two rooms each with a bed for 3 individuals, extra beds are provided.




\ Off Road Trekking \




Indulge into the Vagamon trek. Originating from the base of this resort, the trek will take you to the unseen forests in Vagamon from where you can enjoy fascinating views of the wildlife. Though the trek is comparatively a shorter one, it is an amazing way to get introduced with the immaculate beauty of Vagamon and the untouched natural beauty around it!




\ Coffee Near Lake \




Make your day great with a cup of coffee (or any food you prefer) served near a lake view built in the resort area. You might miss out if you are passing by. Provided with a decent seating and view, it is one of the best services from us that you can get here in our paradise.




\ Farm Visit \




Visit the farm within the resort compound to see the group of animals that are an integral part of the farm inside the resort. The well maintained farm conisits of fish,chicken,cows and goats. If you are a person interersted in farming then this is a great oppurtunity to get to know about farming and its techniques




\ Tree House Stay \




Comprising of 1 Tree house set high above the resort area to provide a beautiful view of the place. The quaint thatch roofs and bamboo walls built by members of indigenous tribes with locally sourced materials, contain all domestic comforts and are designed to utilize natural spring water that flows down from the surroundings hills.Ideal for couples.Due to logistical constraints, guests will be permitted to carry only a limited amount of baggage to the tree house.Please transfer personal items that are absolutely essential for the duration of your stay into a small bag to take with you. The rest of your luggage will be kept safe at the reception and handed over on your checking out from the tree house.




\ Special Monsoon Packages \




The Beauty of Monsoon Season in India. Monsoon is one of the most beautiful season to explorer India, its culture, people, natural beauty and wildlife. The much-awaited monsoon hit the Kerala coast on June 1 and till the end of June or By the first week of July and there are special packages at these time period to explore Vagamon during the most beautiful season.
